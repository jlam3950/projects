About Me

Hi, I'm Jeff L. I first got interested in programming during high school, spending countless hours using Blizzard's map editor to create games utilizing triggers, loops, and different animations. More than anything, I enjoyed the satisfaction of seeing my 'code' come to life. Despite this interest in programming, I decided to enter the healthcare industry, and have been working as a physical therapist for 5 years. In 2020, I decided that I wanted to learn more about the field of computer science, and enrolled in Harvard's CS50 Introduction to Computer Science. I continued self studying before joining Digital Craft's Software Developer bootcamp in 2022.
